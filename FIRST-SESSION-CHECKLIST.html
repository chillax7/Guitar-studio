First Session Checklist

Walk through the app exactly as someone opening it for the very first time would — import a song, split it into stems, mix, plug in a guitar, record a take, and save your work. Each box you tick stays ticked in this browser, so you can close the tab and pick back up later. This needs real audio in and out (a guitar, an interface or built-in mic, speakers or headphones) to mean anything — it's a hands-on pass, not a code review.

Progress is saved to this browser only (localStorage), per device. Nothing is sent anywhere.
